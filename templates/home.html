iSumoK-PseAAC: Identify lysine summoylation sites using Statistical Moments and PseAAC

Description
SUMOylation is a post translational modification that involved in the adaption of cells and functional properties of a large number of proteins. Sumoylation has a key importance in sub cellular concentration, transcriptional synchronization, chromatin remodeling, response to stress, and regulation of mitosis.
In this study, we introduced novel method to predict the Sumolyation sites in the sequences of proteins called as SUMOk-PseAAc with the help of relative position based features by combining the Chou's Pseudo Amino Acid Composition (PseAAC)
